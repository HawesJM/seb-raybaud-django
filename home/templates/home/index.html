Hello there, welcome to my website! With degrees in history and museum studies, I am deeply passionate about arts and culture in general – whether it's the latest museum exhibition or an urban-related show from an emerging artist. With this curious nature, and as a mixed race individual, I am eager to discover more from both sides of the East and West coin. Through these findings, I hope to inspire you — the reader — to learn more one article at a time about the world around us and more specifically, the beauty of Hong Kong itself. Throughout my previous roles, apart from the writing and editing world, I've also done translation and copywriting works that covers a wide range of topics such as art, luxury products and sports. If interested, please do not hesitate to contact me for more information and we can begin this journey together.
